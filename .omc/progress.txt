### Key Learnings
- Architect review caught backtest.py was not in original fix scope — always check all files in a module area
- simulate_trade → execute_decision: decision dict needs "market_type": "btc" explicitly
- Atomic .env: tempfile.mkstemp() preferred over NamedTemporaryFile(delete=False) — fd is returned directly, no double-open
- WebSocket auth: query param `token` not header, because WS handshake doesn't support custom headers in browser
- Settlement expired PnL: paper and live modes must diverge — paper restores stake for realistic simulation
