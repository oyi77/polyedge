# Trades Tab

The Trades tab is a comprehensive ledger of every trade executed by the PolyEdge bot. It allows you to track historical performance, filter by specific trading modes, and evaluate individual trade outcomes.

## What You'll See

This tab is organized into three main sections:
1. **Filter Row**: Controls to narrow down the displayed trade history.
2. **Summary Bar**: Aggregated performance metrics for the currently filtered set.
3. **Trade History Table**: Detailed list of trades with pagination (50 per page).

## Filters

| Filter | Options | Description |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **Mode** | All, Paper, Testnet, Live | Filter trades by the execution environment. |
| **Result** | All, Pending, Win, Loss | Filter trades by their final outcome. |
| **Strategy** | Dropdown | Filter trades generated by a specific trading strategy. |

## Understanding the Data

### Summary Metrics
- **Total Trades**: The count of trades matching your current filters.
- **Wins/Losses**: The distribution of successful vs. unsuccessful trades.
- **Win Rate**: The percentage of settled trades that resulted in a profit.
- **PNL**: The total profit or loss across the filtered set.

### Trade Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Time** | The date and time the trade was executed (local timezone). |
| **Market** | The specific market ticker or event question. |
| **Dir** | The trade direction: UP/YES (predicting a win) or DOWN/NO (predicting a loss). |
| **Size** | The total dollar amount allocated to the trade. |
| **Entry** | The price (in cents) paid for the position. |
| **PNL** | The individual trade profit or loss. |
| **Result** | The final outcome: `win`, `loss`, or `pending`. |
| **Mode** | The environment where the trade was placed (e.g., `LIVE`, `PAPER`). |
| **Strategy** | The name of the automated strategy that initiated the trade. |

## Tips

:::tip
Use the **Strategy Filter** to identify which of your enabled strategies are performing best over time.
:::

:::info
The trades list automatically refreshes every 15 seconds to include the latest executions and settlements.
:::

:::warning
Trades marked as `pending` have not yet been settled by the underlying prediction market.
:::
