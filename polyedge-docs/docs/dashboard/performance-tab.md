# Performance Tab

The Performance tab provides a detailed breakdown of your trading bot's overall success. It's designed to give you a clear picture of your bankroll's growth, strategy effectiveness, and overall system health.

## What You'll See

This tab is organized into multiple sections, providing both high-level and granular insights:
1. **Equity Curve**: A visual representation of your total bankroll over time.
2. **Key Metrics Grid**: A quick-glance view of essential performance indicators.
3. **Strategy P&L Breakdown**: A table detailing individual strategy performance.
4. **Win Rate by Mode**: A bar chart comparing Paper vs. Live trading success.
5. **Strategy Health**: A list of active strategies and their current operational status.
6. **Active Strategies Status**: A summary of enabled vs. disabled strategies and their run intervals.

## Understanding the Data

### Key Metrics
- **Bankroll**: Your current total cash balance.
- **Total PNL**: Your overall profit or loss since starting the bot.
- **Win Rate**: The percentage of settled trades that resulted in a win.
- **Total Trades**: The cumulative count of all executed trades.
- **Avg Trade Size**: The mean dollar amount allocated per trade.
- **Daily PNL**: Your total profit or loss for the current day.

### Strategy P&L Columns

| Column | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Strategy** | The name of the automated strategy (e.g., `BTC Oracle`, `Weather EMOS`). |
| **Trades** | The total number of trades placed by this strategy. |
| **W/L** | The distribution of wins vs. losses for this strategy. |
| **Pend** | The number of currently open trades that haven't settled. |
| **Win%** | The percentage of successful trades for this specific strategy. |
| **PnL** | The total profit or loss generated by this strategy. |

### Strategy Health
- **Status Dot**: Green indicates the strategy is running; red means it is stale or stopped.
- **Last Heartbeat**: The last time the strategy successfully communicated with the system.
- **Lag**: The time delay (in seconds) since the last update.

## Tips

:::tip
Monitor the **Equity Curve** to visualize long-term performance trends and identify periods of significant drawdown.
:::

:::info
The performance data automatically refreshes every 30 seconds to include the latest trade outcomes and equity calculations.
:::

:::warning
Strategies showing a **red status dot** or high **lag** may require administrative attention in the Admin panel.
:::
