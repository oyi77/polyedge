# Trading Terminal

The Trading Terminal is a professional, high-density interface for live market analysis and opportunity detection. It provides a real-time view of market prices, trade opportunities, and whale activity within a single dashboard.

**Route Path:** `/trading-terminal`

## Layout

The page is structured as a responsive, multi-column dashboard:

- **Live Market View**: A grid displaying real-time price data and volume for active Polymarket and Kalshi markets.
- **Opportunity Scanner**: A dedicated panel showing high-conviction trade signals detected by the bot's alpha engines.
- **Whale Activity Feed**: A bottom-mounted wide panel providing a streaming feed of large trades as they happen on-chain.

## Features

- **Live Data**: Real-time WebSocket subscriptions push price updates and order book changes as they happen.
- **Opportunity Detection**: Signals are scored based on edge (the difference between the bot's calculated probability and the market price) and confidence.
- **Whale Activity Monitoring**: Large trades from Polymarket's top traders are streamed into the terminal, allowing for real-time sentiment analysis.
- **Trade Execution**: Users can review detected opportunities and move them into the execution pipeline.

## Data Sources

The Trading Terminal is powered by several real-time data providers:

- **Polymarket WebSocket**: For live price and order book updates.
- **Kalshi API**: For cross-market price comparison and arbitrage opportunities.
- **Alpha Engines**: Signals generated by the 9 core PolyEdge strategies, including BTC Momentum and Weather EMOS.
- **On-chain Events**: Whale activity is derived from real-time monitoring of Polymarket trade events.

:::tip
Use the Opportunity Scanner to identify markets where the bot has detected a significant edge (>5%). These represent the highest-value trading signals.
:::
